### Exercise Set B — Calculator

> **Calculator allowed**

#### Question 1: Speed of Light & Distances in Space

The speed of light is $c = 3.0 \times 10^8$ m/s.

- Distance from Earth to the Sun: $d_1 = 1.5 \times 10^{11}$ m
- Distance from Earth to Proxima Centauri (nearest star): $d_2 = 4.0 \times 10^{16}$ m

**a)** Calculate the time (in seconds) it takes light to travel from the Sun to the Earth. Give your answer in standard form to 2 significant figures.

**b)** How many times further is Proxima Centauri from Earth than the Sun is? Give your answer in standard form.

**c)** Calculate the time it takes light to reach Earth from Proxima Centauri. Give your answer in standard form to 2 significant figures.


## Topic 5 — Significant Figures & Accuracy

> **Calculator allowed**

### Question 1: Significant Figures in Measurement

A rectangular swimming pool has a length of $12.4$ m and a width of $6.8$ m.

**a)** Calculate the area of the pool. Give your answer to **3 significant figures**.

**b)** A fence is built all the way around the pool. Calculate the total length of fencing needed. Give your answer to **2 significant figures**.

**c)** The pool is filled with water to a depth of $1.75$ m. Calculate the volume of water. ($V = \text{length} \times \text{width} \times \text{depth}$). Give your answer to **3 significant figures**.
