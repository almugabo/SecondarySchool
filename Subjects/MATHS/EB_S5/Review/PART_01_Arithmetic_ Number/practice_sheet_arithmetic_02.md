## Topic 1 — Percentages & Reverse Percentages

> **Calculator allowed**

### Question 1: Reverse Percentages — Sale Prices

A sports shop increases all prices by $15\%$ at the start of the season.

A pair of running shoes is sold for **€94.30** after this increase.

**a)** Calculate the original price of the shoes before the increase.

**b)** At the end of the season, the shop reduces the **original** price by $20\%$. Calculate the sale price.

**c)** A customer claims: *"A 15% increase followed by a 20% decrease gives an overall decrease of 5%."*
Is this correct? Show a calculation to justify your answer.

## Topic 5 — Significant Figures & Accuracy

> **Calculator allowed**

### Question 1: Volume and Surface Area of a Cylinder

A cylindrical water tank has a radius of $r = 3.2$ m and a height of $h = 8.5$ m.

Use the formulas: $V = \pi r^2 h$ and $A_{\text{curved}} = 2\pi r h$

**a)** Calculate the volume of the tank. Give your answer to **3 significant figures**.

**b)** Calculate the curved surface area of the tank. Give your answer to **2 significant figures**.

**c)** The tank is filled at a rate of $5.0$ m³ per minute. Calculate the time needed to fill the tank completely. Give your answer to **3 significant figures**.
