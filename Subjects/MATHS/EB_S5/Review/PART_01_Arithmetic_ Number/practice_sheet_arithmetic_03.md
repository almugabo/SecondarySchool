## Topic 2 — Exponential Growth & Compound Interest

### Exercise Set A — No Calculator

> **No calculator**

#### Question 1: Population Growth

The population of a small town is modelled by:

$$P = 20\,000 \times 2^t$$

where $t$ is the number of decades since 2000.

**a)** State the population in the year 2000.

**b)** Complete the table:

| $t$ (decades) | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 |
|---------------|---|---|---|---|
| $P$           |   |   |   |   |

**c)** Estimate the population when $t = 1.5$ (i.e. 15 years after 2000).

**d)** Is this model realistic in the long term? Give a mathematical and a real-world reason.

### Exercise Set B — Calculator

> **Calculator allowed**

#### Question 1: Savings with Compound Interest

Luca deposits €2 400 in a savings account that pays $4.5\%$ interest per year.

The amount after $t$ years is:

$$A = 2400 \times (1.045)^t$$

**a)** Complete the table:

| $t$ (years) | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 |
|-------------|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| $A$ (€)     |   |   |   |   |   |   |

**b)** After how many complete years will Luca have **at least €3 000**?

**c)** A friend deposits €2 400 at $6\%$ interest instead. Write the formula for the friend's amount and state after how many years they will first exceed €3 000.

### Exercise Set B — Calculator

> **Calculator allowed**

#### Question 1: Spacecraft Travel to Mars

- Average distance from Earth to Mars: $d = 2.25 \times 10^{11}$ m
- Speed of spacecraft: $v = 2.5 \times 10^4$ m/s

Use the formula $t = \dfrac{d}{v}$.

**a)** Calculate the travel time in seconds. Give your answer in standard form to **2 significant figures**.

**b)** Convert your answer to days. ($1 \text{ day} = 86\,400 \text{ s}$). Give your answer to **3 significant figures**.

**c)** The spacecraft uses $3.2 \times 10^4$ kg of fuel per day. Calculate the total fuel needed for the journey. Give your answer in standard form to **2 significant figures**.
