## Trigonometry - practice sheet - 01 

### A. Right Triangle Trigonometry
**Exercise 1 — Basic triangle**  
A right triangle has:  
• angle = $35^\circ$  
• adjacent side = 8 cm  
Draw the triangle and find:  
a) the opposite side  
b) the hypotenuse  

**Exercise 2 — Ladder problem**  
A ladder of length 6 m leans against a wall at $60^\circ$.  
Draw the ladder and the wall and find how high the ladder reaches.  

**Exercise 3 — Angle finding**  
In a triangle:  
• opposite = 7  
• adjacent = 10  
Draw the triangle and find $\theta$.  

**Exercise 4 — Mixed**  
A ramp rises 0.5 m over 3 m horizontally.  
Draw the situation and find:  
a) the length of the ramp  
b) the angle of inclination

### D. Trigonometric Functions & Graphs
**Exercise 1 — Identify parameters**  
Given: $y = 3 \sin(2x) - 3.5$  
Find:  
• amplitude  
• period  
• vertical shift  

**Exercise 2 — Graphing**  
Sketch the graph of:  
$y = 2 \cos(x)$  

**Exercise 3 — Real-life model**  
The depth of water is given by:  
$d = 4 \cos\left(\frac{\pi}{6} t + \pi\right) + 10$  
Find:  
a) maximum and minimum depth  
b) period  
c) when $d \ge 9$  

**Exercise 4 — Interpretation**  
A sinusoidal graph has:  
• amplitude = 5  
• midline = 2  
Write a possible equation.
