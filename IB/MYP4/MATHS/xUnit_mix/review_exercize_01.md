### 2. Knowing that the power of a number is repeated multiplication, outline the rules of exponents

| Rule | Name                  | Formula                          |
|------|-----------------------|----------------------------------|
| 1    | Product of Powers     | $$a^m \cdot a^n = a^{m+n}$$       |
| 2    | Quotient of Powers    | $$\frac{a^m}{a^n} = a^{m-n}$$    |
| 3    | Power of a Power      | $$(a^m)^n = a^{m \cdot n}$$      |
| 4    | Identity Exponent     | $$a^1 = a$$                      |
| 5    | Zero Exponent         | $$a^0 = 1$$                      |
| 6    | Negative Exponent     | $$a^{-n} = \frac{1}{a^n}$$        |
| 7    | Power of a Product    | $$(a \cdot b)^n = a^n \cdot b^n$$ |
| 8    | Power of a Quotient   | $$\left(\frac{a}{b}\right)^n = \frac{a^n}{b^n}$$ |

### 6. Solve the Following Problems and Show Your Work

Find the time it takes for the ball to reach the ground.
